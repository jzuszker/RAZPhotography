About

The story behind the lens.

I photograph the in-between moments, the pause before the wave breaks, the held breath of an empty street at dawn. My work strips a scene to its essential geometry and lets light carry the feeling.

Over fifteen years I have shot across thirty countries for editorial, brands, and private collectors. Every print I release is made to last a lifetime on archival paper.
